Perfect Tap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and shared when you use our mobile game, Perfect Tap.

Perfect Tap is a mobile game that may use third-party services such as Google AdMob (for ads) and Google Play Games Services (for optional sign-in and leaderboards).

You can play the game without creating an in-app account. You may optionally sign in with Google Play Games to use leaderboard features.

3. How Information May Be Used
